The Beginning of Everything — My First Breath

Every single thing started when he walked in through our door. Even my life started back then. We were supposed to take a school lesson, but once I saw your beauty from the very first sight, everything changed. Literally, everything. I got anxious because I've never seen a girl that is beauty as much as you. I couldn't believe it. I must confess I didn't understand the word that came out from the teacher. All I was looking for is to look at you once, only once. To memorize all your details so I can dream about you late night, you were and you still stunning, my love.

October 15th, 2022

After Long Time — Fell In Love Again

Before that day, I was frustrated, depressed, and completely lost — because I was losing hope to get you to like me, to love me, to want me. But I was so lucky that we got an invitation call from your mother, to visit you. I brought you a necklace, because your birthday was five days away. I had the best day ever. We chatted for so long. We laughed so much and so hard. And I fell in love all over again — with you, with your beauty, with your smile, with your teeth, with your hair, with your eyes, with every single detail of you that I still remember and memorize from that day. I was so happy from one specific thing that happened: I made you get interested in me by doing some magic tricks for you, and scaring you a little bit. I can't forget that day. I regained all the hope I had lost.

I was convincing you to come to my school just to see you more, chat with you more, and to have the opportunity to look into your eyes every single day. That day, I saw your cheering full soul — and all I could hear was your beautiful voice.

January 1st, 2023

Confession — What The Hell Did I Do?

You trapped me in the corner, you smart animal. You knew there was something telling me to confess my feelings towards you — so you kept pressing on me till I spit it out. And I said it to you. I told you that I like you. When I sent you that message, I turned off my phone, threw it away, and started thinking — what the hell did I do? I told you that I like you!! My heart was racing and I was so anxious. I wanted everything to end in a good way — I didn't want to lose you because I told you that. Fortunately, I didn't... we stayed good friends and neighbors. February 24th, 2023

Writing Our First Page From Our First Book

Two days before, you were sending signals all over the place. I remember you getting bothered because I didn't understand them — actually I did, but I didn't believe them. I thought I was dreaming, or living in my own imagination. But two days later, we were sitting, chilling, listening to music and singing. You reached for the phone to change the song — and all of a sudden you showed me the screen, saying you couldn't find a song named "I Love You." I was shocked to death. No words. Just staring into your eyes and asking you — "Me???" I literally couldn't believe it. I was in actual shock. No words coming out, just happiness and warmth. When I went back home, I looked in the mirror and said: "All of those signals were true. I wasn't dreaming. Everything is a reality." That's when I felt that I had won the entire world — that I was the king of this world.

February 27th, 2023

Our First Date

Our first date. Our first long walk. Our first favorite day. I received a photo of your eyes, crying. You said you were heading to your mom's salon. The moment I saw that photo, I yelled at the taxi driver — "Wait, go back, please!!" I texted you right then: get ready, because I'm taking you out of this mood. Sadness doesn't suit your beautiful face, Princess. I went back and took you. We got there, sat for a bit — then I played our song. "Those Eyes." The song that gives us joy now, and in every single moment we play it. Then we went walking — listening to music, singing, chatting, laughing. It was the best first day ever. That day started so bad, but ended the best way possible.

March 8th, 2023

Living in a Movie — Night Walk

I took you out with me to choose what I'd gift mom for her birthday. We picked a jewelry piece, then headed back home. On the way back, we decided to walk instead of taking a taxi. We were having so much fun — and then it started to rain. It rained on us, right there in the middle of the street. And in that moment, you looked into my eyes and said: "You know in the movies, when it's raining on the main characters, it means they will end up together when the movie ends." To be honest, I didn't know that. But now I know — we will end up together. Hand in hand, no matter what. Then we sat on your threshold, looking up at the sky, the stars, and the moon. It was one of our best days.

The Dream Day

We were hanging out with our siblings at home — watching a football match, having fun. But something felt different. In my heart, and in yours too. Something felt so right. So good. So warming. I didn't know what that feeling was then — but what I discovered after is that we felt it together. Despite our siblings being right there, we kept loving each other. Eye contact, every love language, laughing at each other's jokes, focusing on no one but each other. That day was so special — to me and to you. That day, we felt that we were going to choose each other. That we were going to end up together. And here we are.

Chapter III · My Anniversary Letter

Happy Anniversary, My Lovely Princess

Despite of writing this letter, These words are a small token of the love and appreciation I have for you.

My Lovely Princess,


As I sit here writing this letter I keep wondering how I can possibly fit entire years of memories, emotions, laughter, challenges, photos, and love into few pages. The truth is I probably can't. No words could ever fully capture what you mean to me or how much my life has changed since you walked through that door and brightened my world and shocked my heart.


When I think about us in those 3 years, I don't only think about our big moments. I think about the small ones that nobody else can ever see in us. The blushing faces we have when we look to each other, the conversations that lasted more than they should have, the moments I hear your voice after a difficult day and somehow everything feels lighter, the times when a simple message from you changes my mood, the way my heart would react whenever I saw your name appearing on my screen.


Before you, I never understood how one person could become a whole world, goals, dreams in someone else's world. Yet somehow, without realizing it, you became my first thought whenever I open my eyes after deep sleeping, and my last thought whenever I go to sleep after a long day, as well as my dreams and hopes for the future, and my happiest memories. You are my last 7 minutes of living.


If someone had told me on the day we first met and you melted my heart that I one day would be writing an anniversary letter to you, I would never have believed them. Yet here I am, grateful for every smile, every challenge we overcame, every memory we created, every dream we built.


There were times when things weren't easy for us. The distances, circumstances, or uncertainty made everything feel complicated. There were times that I missed you more than I knew how to express. Moments that all I needed was your voice, your face in front of mine, your loud laugh, and spending few minutes with you. But now, I realized these difficult moments taught us how much we mean to each other, how much our love is strong, and how much our relationship means to us.


You have seen sides of me that many people never seen and never will. You have been there for me during moments of confidence and moments of doubt. You have celebrated my successes and stood beside me during difficult days. Whether you realize it or not, your presence has given me strength more times than I can count.


What I love most about you isn't just one thing, it's even impossible to count but I will tell you some. It's the way you care. The way you make me feel valued. Your appreciation for me. The way your voice can calm my mind. The way your smile can brighten a day that seemed impossible to fix. It's the combination of countless things that together make you completely unforgettable.


Sometimes I wonder if you know your true value. If I could give you one thing, it would be the ability to see yourself through my eyes. Then you would understand how special you are, how deeply you are loved, how much of an impact you have had on my life, how beautiful you are, how you can be 5 different personalities in your only personality — my girlfriend, my daughter, my mom, my friend, my sister. And the last thing you would see is how I am planning to make you live, like what you actually deserve from me and from this life.


Today isn't just a celebration of another year together. It's a celebration for every moment that brought us here. Every memory, every laugh, every conversation, and every feeling that helped us share that bond we had created.


Thank you. Seriously thank you. Thank you for being my comfort, refuge, home during difficult times, my happiness during good times, and my favorite person ever through every life's seasons. Thank you for every memory we shared and created and for every moment we have yet to create.


No matter what the future holds, I know one thing for certain: meeting you 5 years ago was the best thing that ever happened in my life, and no matter what I'll keep working on our future, our journey, our relationship.


Happy Anniversary, my beautiful baby.

I Love You.
